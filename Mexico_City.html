Mexico City, Mexico

Why Visit Mexico City?

Mexico City is a vibrant capital with rich history, museums, markets, and amazing food. From ancient ruins to modern neighborhoods, there’s always something to explore.

Booking Information

Average Flight Cost: $250 – $500 round trip

Hotel Range: $80 – $200 per night

Best Travel Months: March – May & October – November

Recommended Activities: Historic center, museums, street food, Teotihuacán pyramids.
