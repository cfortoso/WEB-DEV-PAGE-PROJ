Our Story

Exploriva Travel was founded in 2011 by a team of travelers who believed planning a trip should be exciting — not stressful. They envisioned a platform where anyone could compare flights, stays, and experiences with total clarity.

What began as a simple travel search engine has now grown into a full travel discovery platform used by people worldwide. Our mission remains the same: make exploring the world simpler, smarter, and more inspiring.

Our Mission

Exploriva Travel exists to make travel effortless and meaningful. We believe every traveler deserves honest information, transparent prices, and tools that help them make confident decisions.

How Exploriva Became What It Is


  • 2023 — The Beginning
    Exploriva officially launches with the mission to make modern travel simpler, smarter, and more transparent.
  • 2024 — Hotels & Stays Expanded
    Hotel and stay comparisons are introduced, creating a unified platform for flights, accommodations, and planning.
  • 2025 — Experiences & Activities Added
    Guided tours, cultural activities, and adventure experiences are added for complete itinerary planning.
