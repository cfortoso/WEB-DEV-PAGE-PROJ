Venice, Italy

Why Visit Venice?

Venice is a charming canal city known for its gondolas, historic bridges, and romantic atmosphere. Landmarks like St. Mark’s Square and the Rialto Bridge make it one of Italy’s most iconic destinations.

Booking Information

Average Flight Cost: $600 – $900 round trip

Hotel Range: $120 – $250 per night

Best Travel Months: April – June & September

Recommended Activities: Gondola rides, canal tours, museums, Italian cuisine.
