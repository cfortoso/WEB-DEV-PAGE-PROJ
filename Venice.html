Venice

The Floating City of Romance

Why Visit Venice?

Venice is a magical canal city filled with gondolas, historic bridges, and unforgettable views. Wander through narrow alleyways, explore St. Mark’s Square, and float through iconic waterways on a gondola ride.


Whether you're exploring islands like Burano and Murano or enjoying Italian cuisine by the water, Venice is one of Europe's most enchanting destinations.

Trip Planner

Best Season: April – June & September

Flight Cost: $600 – $900

Top Activities: Gondola ride, canal tour, St. Mark’s Basilica
