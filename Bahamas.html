Bahamas

Why Visit the Bahamas?

The Bahamas offers crystal-clear waters, soft sandy beaches, and a relaxed island vibe. It’s perfect for swimming, snorkeling, and spending time at oceanfront resorts.

Booking Information

Average Flight Cost: $350 – $650 round trip

Hotel Range: $150 – $350 per night

Best Travel Months: December – April

Recommended Activities: Beach days, snorkeling, boat tours, island hopping.
