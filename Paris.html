Paris, France

Why Visit Paris?

Paris is known as the City of Light, famous for its art, culture, fashion, and landmarks like the Eiffel Tower and the Louvre Museum.

Booking Information

Average Flight Cost: $700 – $1,100 round trip

Hotel Range: $120 – $250 per night

Best Travel Months: April – June & September – October

Recommended Activities: Eiffel Tower, museums, Seine river cruise, cafés.
