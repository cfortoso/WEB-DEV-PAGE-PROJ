Travel Tips & Essentials

Packing Checklist

  • Passport, ID, travel documents
  • Comfortable walking shoes
  • Portable charger & cables
  • Weather-appropriate clothes
  • Reusable water bottle
  • Travel-size toiletries

Flight Tips

  • Arrive 2–3 hours before international flights
  • Keep essentials in your carry-on
  • Download offline maps & entertainment
  • Set alarms for connecting flights

Safety Tips

  • Keep copies of important documents
  • Use hotel safes when possible
  • Stay aware in crowded places
  • Avoid keeping all money in one place
