Tokyo, Japan

Why Visit Tokyo?

Tokyo blends tradition with modern technology. Experience neon nightlife, temples, anime culture, and world-famous food—all in one city.

Booking Information

Average Flight Cost: $900 – $1500 round trip

Hotel Range: $80 – $200 per night

Best Travel Months: March–May & October–November

Recommended Activities: Shibuya Crossing, Tokyo Tower, temples, sushi markets.
