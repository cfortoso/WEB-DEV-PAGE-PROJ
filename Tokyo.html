Why Visit Tokyo?

Tokyo is a city where ancient temples stand beside futuristic skyscrapers. Explore peaceful shrines like Senso-ji in Asakusa, shop for anime and electronics in Akihabara, and walk across the famous Shibuya Crossing under glowing billboards.

Spend your days visiting Tokyo Skytree or Tokyo Tower for panoramic city views, relaxing in Ueno Park, and trying fresh sushi at Toyosu Market. In the evenings, discover ramen shops, themed cafés, karaoke, and vibrant nightlife in neighborhoods like Shinjuku and Shibuya.

Trip Planner

Best Season: March – May (cherry blossoms), October – November (fall colors)

Flight Cost: $900 – $1500

Hotel Cost: $80 – $200 / night

Top Activities: Shibuya Crossing, Senso-ji Temple, Akihabara anime district, Tokyo Skytree, Harajuku shopping, Odaiba waterfront
